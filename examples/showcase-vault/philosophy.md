# Philosophy

Mycora's design rests on a small number of principles that
constrain almost every other decision in the app.

- [[The tree model]] — every note has exactly one parent, full stop
- [[The mycelial layer]] — cross-links as a separate, many-to-many relation
- [[Markdown as source of truth]] — nothing about your data should require
  Mycora to remain readable

Two note-taking traditions dominate today, and almost nothing in the
terminal does both well: hierarchical outliners (Workflowy, Dendron) give
structure but isolate notes outside their branch; Zettelkasten/graph tools
(Obsidian, Roam, `zk`) give free association but no strict place to anchor
yourself. Mycora's bet is that these aren't actually in tension — see
[[The tree model]] and [[The mycelial layer]] for how it holds both at
once.
