# Architecture

- [[One binary, four entry points]] — `terapi`, `terapi run`, `terapi
  build`, `terapi import`
- [[campaign.rs is the one engine behind three different surfaces]]
- [[Variable resolution follows a strict priority chain]]
- [[Async work reports back over per-concern channels]]
- [[External tools are env-var-gated, with sane defaults]]
