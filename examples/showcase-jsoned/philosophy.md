# Philosophy

Jsoned's README makes its positioning a literal comparison table
against `jless`, `fx`, `jq`, and `difftastic`/`delta` across
View/Edit/Convert/Diff/Pipe-mode columns — jsoned is the only one with
a check in every column. The ROADMAP's own stated vision: "a fast,
keyboard-first TUI that lets you read, edit, and convert JSON without
leaving the terminal — and without installing a GUI editor just to
change one value."

- [[An external editor, not just a standalone tool]] — the
  `TERAPI_JSON_EDITOR=jsoned` integration goal
- [[Structural diff, not textual diff]] — why it doesn't compete with
  difftastic/delta
